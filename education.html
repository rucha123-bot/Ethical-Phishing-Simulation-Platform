This was a simulated phishing attack for training purposes.

If this were a real attack, your credentials could have been stolen.

How to Spot a Phishing Email:

  • Check the Sender: Is the email address legitimate? Hover over it to see the real address.
  • Look for Urgency: Attackers create a sense of urgency to make you act without thinking.
  • Hover, Don't Click: Hover over links to see the actual URL they lead to.
  • Spelling & Grammar: Professional companies usually don't have major errors.
  • Generic Greetings: "Dear Customer" instead of your name can be a red flag.

Stay vigilant and always think before you click!
